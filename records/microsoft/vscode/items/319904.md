The "Open in Integrated Terminal" and "Open Folder" strings use proper `nls.localize()` calls in the source. Missing Chinese translations are a language pack content issue — the Chinese Language Pack extension (`ms-ceintl.vscode-language-pack-zh-hans`) is responsible for providing these translations.
