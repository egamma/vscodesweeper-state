# Issue #258446 — Checkpoint - Undo Last Request/Redo Last Request

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/258446](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/258446)

## Summary

Team-authored UX feedback on the granularity of Undo/Redo Last Request vs. Restore Checkpoint in chat editing sessions — the author expects undo to operate per-turn, not per-request within a turn. Keeping open per team-authored policy; the discussion between team members is still unresolved.

## Change summary

The 'Undo Last Request' action in chat undoes individual requests within a turn rather than undoing the entire turn, which is inconsistent with the checkpoint/turn mental model.

## Best solution

Align the undo/redo granularity so that 'Undo Last Request' operates on a full turn (matching 'Restore Checkpoint' behavior), or clarify the UX distinction between the two actions. The relevant logic lives in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/actions/chatExecuteActions.ts` and the checkpoint timeline in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/chatEditing/chatEditingCheckpointTimelineImpl.ts`. Needs a product decision from the chat team on intended semantics.
