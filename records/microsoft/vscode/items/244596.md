# Issue #244596 — Not clear why reporting feedback is under `telemetry`

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/244596](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/244596)

## Summary

Team-authored design discussion questioning why the feedback setting lives under the `telemetry` namespace. The setting was renamed from `telemetry.disableFeedback` to `telemetry.feedback.enabled` (in `src/vs/platform/telemetry/common/telemetryService.ts`), but the namespace concern remains unresolved. Hard keep-open: author is MEMBER.

## Change summary

The feedback-related setting (`telemetry.feedback.enabled`) is registered under the `telemetry` namespace, which is conceptually misaligned since feedback (issue reporter, surveys) is not the same as telemetry.

## Best solution

Move the `telemetry.feedback.enabled` setting to a more appropriate namespace (e.g. `workbench.feedback.enabled`) and update all consumers in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/issue/`, `src/vs/workbench/contrib/surveys/`, `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/actions/chatTitleActions.ts`, and the policy conversion tests. Requires a migration for existing user settings.
