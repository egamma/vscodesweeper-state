# Issue #253436 — Moving lines causes rendering to flash

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/253436](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/253436)

## Summary

Team-authored issue reporting a visual flicker when moving lines; already under investigation by a team member who identified the root cause. Must stay open per team-author policy.

## Change summary

Moving lines with Alt+Up/Down causes a rendering flash because decorations are deleted and re-added asynchronously rather than moved atomically.

## Best solution

Implement an atomic move-line operation that carries decorations to the new position without an intermediate delete/re-add cycle, avoiding the flicker caused by the typescript service recalculating the outline model between steps.
