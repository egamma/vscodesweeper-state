# Issue #196943 — cursorUp/down behavior inconsistent when moving from selection

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/196943](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/196943)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: moveUp/moveDown with an active selection collapse to the selection's start/end boundary respectively, ignoring which end holds the caret. The team has labeled it under-discussion — a design decision is still pending.

## Change summary

Cursor up/down after a selection jumps to the wrong column when the caret is at the opposite end from the direction of movement.

## Best solution

Decide whether to change moveUp/moveDown to collapse to the caret position (cursor.position) rather than selection start/end, then update `src/vs/editor/common/cursor/cursorMoveOperations.ts` lines 207–214 and 252–259 accordingly. This is a design call on expected behavior with possible compatibility impact.
