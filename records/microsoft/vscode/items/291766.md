[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/291766](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/291766)

## Summary

Valid bug: double-clicking an in-item toggle in the quick pick fires the tree's selection (accept) handler instead of toggling twice. Team-authored issue — keeping open for maintainer judgment.

## Change summary

Double-clicking a toggle button inside a quick pick item selects/accepts the item rather than toggling the button twice.

## Best solution

The click handler on the label element (line ~356 in quickInputList.ts) should call `e.preventDefault()` or `e.stopPropagation()` when the click target is the toggle/checkbox, preventing the tree's selection-on-double-click from firing. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/platform/quickinput/browser/quickInputList.ts` in the `QuickPickItemElementRenderer` template setup.
