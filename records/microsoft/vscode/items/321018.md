# Issue #321018 — Output dropdown closes when the list updates

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/321018](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/321018)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: when a new output channel registers during startup, the menu fires onDidChange, which triggers a full toolbar rebuild (ViewPane.updateActions → setActions), disposing and recreating the SubmenuEntrySelectActionViewItem and closing the dropdown.

## Change summary

The Output channel selector dropdown closes every time a new channel registers, making it unusable during startup.

## Best solution

Either debounce/batch the menu update during rapid channel registration, or make the SubmenuEntrySelectActionViewItem update its options in-place (via setOptions) instead of being disposed and recreated when the underlying submenu changes. The fix likely lives in the toolbar/action-bar layer's reaction to menu changes, or in the output contribution's channel registration pattern.
