# Issue #279464 — The completions in the front matter is influenced by the space after the `:`

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/279464](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/279464)

## Summary

Team-authored issue still unfixed on main. The completion widget refilters suggestions when a space is typed after the colon in front matter, causing confusing narrowing of valid options. jrieken suggested adding a trailing space to each filterText property as a fix, but no change has landed.

## Change summary

Completions in prompt file front matter are incorrectly filtered when the space after `:` is included in the completion range, hiding valid options.

## Best solution

In `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/common/promptSyntax/languageProviders/promptHeaderAutocompletion.ts`, ensure the completion range starts after the space following the colon (or add a trailing space to each `filterText`), so typing the space doesn't refilter the list. Per jrieken's comment, appending a space to filterText values is the simplest path.
