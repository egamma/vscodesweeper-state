# Issue #279464 — The completions in the front matter is influenced by the space after the `:`

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/279464](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/279464)

## Summary

Team-authored issue (MEMBER) — must keep open. The bug is real and a concrete fix direction exists (trailing space in filterText), but no fix has landed yet.

## Change summary

Completions in chat front matter are incorrectly filtered after typing a space following `:`, showing only a subset of valid completions.

## Best solution

Add a trailing space to the `filterText` property of completion items provided for front matter values, so that the space after `:` does not cause the suggest widget to re-rank/filter the list unexpectedly. The fix likely belongs in the chat-prompts completion provider.
