Assigned to 'On Deck' milestone — a real scheduling decision that mandates keep-open. The design question (how to handle providers that never resolve) remains unaddressed on current main; no timeout or incremental-results mechanism was added to the search provider flow.

## Change summary

Search find widget stalls when a provider never resolves its promise; needs a strategy to show results incrementally or time out bad providers.

## Best solution

Implement a timeout or incremental-results strategy in the search provider orchestration (likely in `src/vs/workbench/services/search/` and the find widget) so that results from responsive providers surface even when one provider hangs. The design decision (timeout vs. streaming vs. cancellation) needs maintainer input.
