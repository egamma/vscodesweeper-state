# Issue #206409 — Hover over quick pick

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/206409](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/206409)

## Summary

Team-authored polish issue about hover tooltips rendering on top of the quick pick overlay — hard keep-open rule applies. The discussion between maintainers is still open on the desired behavior.

## Change summary

Hover tooltips from underlying UI elements (e.g. explorer) can pop over the quick pick, creating a z-order layering issue.

## Best solution

Dismiss or suppress hovers when the quick pick (or any overlay widget at a higher z-layer) is open, likely in the hover scheduling logic in `src/vs/base/browser/ui/hover/` or the quick pick's focus/show path. The open question from @benibenj about whether hiding hovers beneath the quick pick might itself look buggy needs a maintainer decision.
