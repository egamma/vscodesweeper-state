## Summary

Still unfixed on main: no commits address the worktree symbol leakage or the readonly marking requested here. The bug is confirmed by a team member with clear repro steps and belongs in the agent-host/scm area.

## Change summary

Open Workspace Symbol picker shows symbols from agent-session worktree directories, tricking users into opening and editing worktree files.

## Best solution

Two fixes needed: (1) exclude agent-session worktree folders from workspace symbol search so they don't appear in the picker, and (2) mark worktree working directories as readonly via `IFilesConfigurationService` dynamic session overrides (with glob support as discussed in the comments). The filesConfiguration service at `src/vs/workbench/services/filesConfiguration/common/filesConfigurationService.ts` already supports dynamic overrides but needs glob-based patterns; the search exclusion likely involves the workspace symbol provider or search service.
