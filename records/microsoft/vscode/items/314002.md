## Summary

Already implemented: VS Code detects agentic CLIs (Claude, Codex, Copilot, Gemini) and uses their escape-sequence title for the terminal tab, controlled by the `terminal.integrated.tabs.allowAgentCliTitle` setting (default: true). Shipped in 1.117.0.
