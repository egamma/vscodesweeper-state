# Issue #105302 — Hover not showing on inline decorations

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/105302](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/105302)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: zero-width decorations with `renderOptions.after.contentText` don't trigger hover because the hover participant uses the decoration's literal range (which is zero-width) rather than extending it to cover the visually rendered inline content.

## Change summary

Hovering over inline after-content decorations with a zero-width range does not show the decoration's hoverMessage.

## Best solution

Extend the hover range computation in `markdownHoverParticipant.ts` to account for decorations that have `afterContentClassName` (or `after` renderOptions) on a zero-width range, so the hover target covers the visually rendered after-content. The mouse target resolution in `mouseTarget.ts` may also need adjustment to resolve hover over after-content spans back to the decoration's position.
