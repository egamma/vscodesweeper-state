# Issue #262483 — Search tab starts iterating over results on its own

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/262483](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/262483)

## Summary

Intermittent bug where search results auto-cycle focus without user input, confirmed with extensions disabled. A team member engaged but the reproduction is inconsistent, and no root cause or fix has been identified on current main. Keeping open for further investigation.

## Change summary

The Search view automatically iterates through results on its own, especially after switching away from and back to VS Code.

## Best solution

Investigate the focus/selection event handlers in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/search/browser/searchView.ts` — the `onDidChangeFocus` and `onDidOpen` debounced listeners (lines ~1021–1038) could cascade when the tree regains focus after an app switch. A maintainer should attempt reproduction on Linux/Fedora and trace whether a spurious focus-change cycle is triggered by the window regain event.
