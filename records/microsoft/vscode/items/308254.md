## Summary

Still reproduces on main: line 80 of terminal.links.contribution.ts subscribes to `onDidRemoveLinkProvider` outside the `isDetachedTerminalInstance` guard, leaking a listener per detached terminal onto the singleton emitter. The related fix PR #310157 addressed other terminal leaks but missed this one.

## Change summary

Detached terminals leak a listener on `onDidRemoveLinkProvider` because the subscription at line 80 is not guarded by the `isDetachedTerminalInstance` check.

## Best solution

Move line 80's `onDidRemoveLinkProvider` subscription inside the existing `if (!isDetachedTerminalInstance(...))` block (lines 72–79) so detached terminals never subscribe to the singleton link-provider emitter.
