## Change summary

Improve visual distinction between quickpick item labels and their descriptions, which currently blend together especially in light themes.

## Best solution

The team is evaluating options (separator dot, increased spacing, font-weight changes). Let the design discussion conclude, then implement the chosen approach in `src/vs/base/browser/ui/iconLabel/iconlabel.css` and/or `src/vs/platform/quickinput/browser/media/quickInput.css`.
