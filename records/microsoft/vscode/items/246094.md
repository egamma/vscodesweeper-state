# Issue #246094 — Leake disposable: RangesLimitReporter

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/246094](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/246094)

## Summary

Still reproduces on current main. The milestone is 'On Deck' (a scheduling milestone), and the leak pattern is confirmed in source. The disposable subscription to `info.onDidChange` in `LanguageStatusEntry.onActiveEditorChanged` is not disposed when the underlying limit reporter (and its Emitter) is disposed before the next editor change.

## Change summary

Event subscription to RangesLimitReporter/DecoratorLimitReporter's `onDidChange` leaks when the reporter is disposed before the LanguageStatusEntry cleans up its listener.

## Best solution

In `LanguageStatusEntry.onActiveEditorChanged`, after obtaining `info`, also listen to the editor's `onDidDispose` (or a similar lifecycle signal from the limit reporter) to dispose `_indicatorChangeListener` eagerly when the source is torn down. Alternatively, make the subscription auto-dispose via the editor's lifecycle store. Fix lives in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/limitIndicator/browser/limitIndicator.contribution.ts`.
