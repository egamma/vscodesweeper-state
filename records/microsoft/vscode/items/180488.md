# Issue #180488 — DAP error response handling is confusing and inconsistent

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/180488](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/180488)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the `handleErrorResponse` method in `rawDebugSession.ts` ignores `showUser` when a `url` is present (line 802), and the interaction between `showErrors` and `showUser` remains confusing and inconsistent. A team member (roblourens) acknowledged this as a bug in April 2023 but no fix has landed.

## Change summary

DAP error response handling ignores `showUser=false` when a URL is provided, and the `showErrors`/`showUser` interaction produces confusing and inconsistent UI behavior (empty dialogs, unexpected notifications).

## Best solution

Refactor `handleErrorResponse` in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/debug/browser/rawDebugSession.ts` to respect `showUser` independently of the `url` property, and clarify the `showErrors` parameter's role — potentially distinguishing critical vs non-critical requests to decide between modal dialogs and notifications. The DAP spec discussion (microsoft/debug-adapter-protocol#387) should inform the final behavior.
