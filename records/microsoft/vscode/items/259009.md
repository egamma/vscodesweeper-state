- **prior similar issues:** https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/221237 and https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/281458 reported the same error message; the former was a known regression (https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/220999) fixed in July 2024, the latter closed without resolution.

The issue likely lies in the Inno Setup installer or `inno_updater` binary rather than the VS Code source.
