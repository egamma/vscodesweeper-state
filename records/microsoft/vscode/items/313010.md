## Summary

Likely a real UI bug: the 'Use for New Windows' checkbox state is not correctly reflected when reopening the profiles editor for non-default profiles, though the underlying setting persists. No fix has been applied on main. Related to existing team-filed issue #255995 but distinct in symptom.

## Change summary

The 'Use for New Windows' checkbox in the Profiles editor visually reverts to unchecked for non-default profiles after closing and reopening the settings, even though the setting is persisted and respected functionally.
