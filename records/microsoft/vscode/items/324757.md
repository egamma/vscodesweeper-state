# Issue #324757 — "Create PR" and "Mark as Done" both appear

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/324757](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/324757)

## Summary

Team-authored bug with an assigned milestone (1.131.0) — keeping open. The issue is that both 'Create PR' (extension-contributed) and 'Mark as Done' (core) appear simultaneously in the AgentsChangesToolbar, likely due to incomplete mutual exclusion in their `when` clauses.

## Change summary

Both 'Create PR' and 'Mark as Done' buttons appear simultaneously in the agents window Changes toolbar when they should be mutually exclusive.

## Best solution

Fix the `when`-clause mutual exclusion between the extension-contributed 'Create PR' action and the core 'Mark as Done' action on `MenuId.AgentsChangesToolbar`. The 'Mark as Done' action's `when` clause in `sessionsViewActions.ts` (~line 1079) shows it appears when `HasBranchChanges` is false OR when merge/PR conditions are met — the 'Create PR' action (contributed by the Copilot extension) likely overlaps in the PR-exists + protected-branch state. The fix should ensure these two states are truly disjoint.
