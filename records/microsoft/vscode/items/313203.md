# Issue #313203 — Search and replace sometimes leaves replacement above viewport when collapse unchanged regions is enabled

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/313203](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/313203)

## Summary

Legitimate bug report about viewport/scroll misbehavior when using search-and-replace with diff editor's 'collapse unchanged regions' enabled. No fix found on current main. Needs routing to the diff-editor and search-replace area owners.

## Change summary

After applying a search-and-replace match with 'collapse unchanged regions' enabled, the viewport shows mostly blank space or the replaced content becomes unreachable above the viewport.

## Best solution

The diff editor's hideUnchangedRegionsFeature (src/vs/editor/browser/widget/diffEditor/features/hideUnchangedRegionsFeature.ts) likely needs to ensure the viewport scrolls to reveal the replacement after a replace operation, or the search-replace flow needs to expand the relevant collapsed region before/after replacement.
