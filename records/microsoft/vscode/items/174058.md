# Issue #174058 — Rendered whitespace characters non-uniform

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/174058](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/174058)

## Summary

Still present on main: SVG-based whitespace rendering (the default) produces non-uniform dot brightness at negative zoom levels due to sub-pixel coordinate rounding. No fix has landed; the workaround is setting `editor.experimentalWhitespaceRendering` to `"font"` or `"off"`.

## Change summary

Rendered whitespace dots appear with varying brightness/dithering at negative zoom levels when using SVG-based whitespace rendering.

## Best solution

Fix the SVG viewBox or circle coordinates in `src/vs/editor/browser/viewParts/whitespace/whitespace.ts` to snap to whole pixels at fractional zoom levels, or consider switching the default rendering method away from SVG for affected configurations.
