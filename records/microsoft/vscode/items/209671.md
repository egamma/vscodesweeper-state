# Issue #209671 — diffs are barely visible in HC light theme

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/209671](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/209671)

## Summary

Team-authored accessibility bug that remains unfixed on main. The dirty diff gutter decorations in HC Light theme rely on thin border-left indicators whose colors, while explicitly set, produce low contrast against the white editor background — especially problematic for a high-contrast theme whose purpose is maximizing visibility.

## Change summary

Dirty diff (SCM quick diff) gutter decorations are barely visible in the High Contrast Light theme.

## Best solution

Increase the visual weight of dirty diff gutter decorations in HC Light — either by using wider/bolder indicators, choosing higher-contrast colors against the white background, or both. The fix would touch `src/vs/workbench/contrib/scm/browser/media/dirtydiffDecorator.css` (decoration width/styling for HC) and potentially `src/vs/workbench/contrib/scm/common/quickDiff.ts` or `src/vs/workbench/services/themes/common/workbenchThemeService.ts` (HC Light color values).
