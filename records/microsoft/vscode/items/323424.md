# Issue #323424 — Credits do not add up nicely

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/323424](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/323424)

## Summary

Team-authored bug report — the session-level credit total does not include subagent credits, confirmed by a community reporter. The `sessionCost` getter in `chatModel.ts` sums only `request.response.usage.copilotCredits` per turn, but depending on how the agent host reports usage, subagent credits may be tracked separately (via `subAgentCreditsAccumulator`) and not folded back into the per-turn `copilotCredits` field. Keeping open for the author to address.

## Change summary

The session info hover shows a total credit cost that excludes credits consumed by subagents, making the displayed total lower than the actual sum of individual turn costs.

## Best solution

Ensure `sessionCost` in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/common/model/chatModel.ts` (line ~2336) accounts for subagent credits — either by ensuring the agent host always folds subagent usage into the parent turn's `copilotCredits`, or by having `sessionCost` also sum subagent-specific credits tracked on tool invocations. The context-usage widget and hover in `chatContextUsageDetails.ts` would then show correct totals without further changes.
