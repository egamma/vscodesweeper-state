# Issue #228367 — Multifile diff editor leaks models and editor after closing and hiding

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/228367](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/228367)

## Summary

Still present on main: after discarding the refactor preview, `UXState.restore` only closes editors with the `vscode-bulkeditpreview-multieditor` scheme but does not close/hide individual diff editors using the `vscode-bulkeditpreview-editor` scheme, leaving them visible in `visibleTextEditors`. The 'On Deck' milestone triggers a hard keep-open rule.

## Change summary

After closing/discarding the refactor preview, editors and models with the `vscode-bulkeditpreview-editor://` scheme leak and remain in `visibleTextEditors`.

## Best solution

In `UXState.restore` (or the `_done` flow in `BulkEditPane`), also close editors whose resource scheme is `BulkEditPreviewProvider.Schema` (`vscode-bulkeditpreview-editor`), or ensure model references created via `_getOrCreatePreviewModel` are fully released and corresponding editors are hidden when the session ends.
