# Issue #225945 — Error: `No view is registered with id` might be caused by a race condition

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/225945](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/225945)

## Summary

The race condition is still present on current main: `$registerTreeViewDataProvider` resolves after `whenInstalledExtensionsRegistered()` but the view may not yet be in the ViewsRegistry during extension restart-after-update, producing the error at line 70 of mainThreadTreeViews.ts.

## Change summary

After updating and restarting an extension with async activation, tree views may fail to register because the ViewsRegistry lookup fires before view contributions are re-registered.

## Best solution

Guard the data-provider registration to either retry/wait for the view descriptor to appear in the ViewsRegistry, or defer registration until the view is available — the fix belongs in `src/vs/workbench/api/browser/mainThreadTreeViews.ts` around line 49–71.
