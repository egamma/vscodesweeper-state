## Summary

Team-authored feature request for the diff editor to highlight entire lines when they are mostly different rather than aligning them with inline char-level diffs. No implementation found on current main; keeping open per team-member rule.

## Change summary

Lines in the diff editor that are mostly different should be shown as full-line insertions/deletions rather than aligned with misleading inline character changes.

## Best solution

Enhance the diff editor's alignment/rendering logic (likely in the diff view zones or the diff computer's output mapping) to detect when aligned lines have very low similarity and switch to full-line highlighting instead of char-level decorations. The threshold or heuristic could be a configurable ratio of changed characters to line length.
