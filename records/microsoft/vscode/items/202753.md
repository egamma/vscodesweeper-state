## Change summary

When using global find-replace with `diffEditor.hideUnchangedRegions.enabled`, the replace preview diff scrolls off-screen after unchanged regions collapse, hiding the actual change.

## Best solution

The diff editor's hideUnchangedRegions feature should ensure the viewport stays anchored to the relevant change after collapsing. The fix likely belongs in `src/vs/editor/browser/widget/diffEditor/features/hideUnchangedRegionsFeature.ts` — when regions collapse, the scroll position should be adjusted to keep the revealed change visible.
