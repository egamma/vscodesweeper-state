# Issue #96643 — Tasks (and TaskExecutions) are not === in the onDid(Start|End)Task callbacks.

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/96643](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/96643)

## Summary

Fixed on main in May 2020 and shipped in 1.46.0; the reporter confirmed the fix works. This can be closed as completed.

## Change summary

Task and TaskExecution objects were not === comparable in onDidStartTaskProcess/onDidEndTaskProcess callbacks, breaking extension logic that depended on identity checks.

## Best solution

Keep the shipped fix (commit 230a2293df1, released in 1.46.0); no further change needed.
