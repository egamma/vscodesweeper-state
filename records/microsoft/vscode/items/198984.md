This race condition — where the wrong sidebar view appears after reload in remote/WSL — is no longer reproducible. The reporter confirmed it didn't happen on Insiders in 2023, and the nearly identical issue #245936 was closed in June 2025 as 'no longer reproducing'. Fixes to the pane composite opening logic (race condition guard in 9618a72f164) likely resolved this.
