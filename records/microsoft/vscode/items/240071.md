# Issue #240071 — "Relaunch Terminal" is sometimes cut out and not visible

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/240071](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/240071)

## Summary

Fixed on main — the hover action bar overflow was caused by missing `box-sizing: border-box` on the status bar actions container, fixed in commit 59e50e443d0 and shipped in 1.122.0.

## Change summary

The 'Relaunch Terminal' button in the terminal tab hover tooltip is sometimes cut off and not visible due to CSS overflow.

## Best solution

Keep the shipped fix (commit 59e50e443d0, 1.122.0); no further change needed. The `box-sizing: border-box` on `.hover-row.status-bar .actions` prevents padding from pushing action buttons outside the container.
