# Issue #41574 — Can we get a few more pixels for clicking the cursor at the beginning of a line?

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/41574](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/41574)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the narrow click target at the beginning of a line remains unaddressed — there is no left padding option and no expanded hit-test area for the leftmost pixels of the content zone. Valid bug worth keeping open.

## Change summary

Clicking at the very beginning of a line to place the cursor is unreliable because the clickable area is only the left half of the first character, making it easy to accidentally hit the folding gutter or land at column 2.

## Best solution

Widen the mouse hit-test area at the left edge of the content zone in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/mouseTarget.ts` so that clicks in the first few pixels to the right of the gutter reliably resolve to column 1. Alternatively, add an `editor.padding.left` setting (as requested in https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/93053) that gives a clickable buffer zone. The fix likely lives in the `BareHitTestRequest` / `MouseTargetFactory` hit-testing logic.
