# Issue #41574 — Can we get a few more pixels for clicking the cursor at the beginning of a line?

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/41574](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/41574)

## Summary

Still present on main: the click target at the beginning of a line is very narrow (first half of first character), and no fix or left-padding mechanism has been introduced. The 'On Deck' milestone also triggers the hard keep-open rule.

## Change summary

Clicking at the very beginning of a line is frustratingly narrow — the cursor I-beam is shown but clicks often miss and land in the gutter or on column 2.

## Best solution

Widen the hit-test area at the left edge of the content area in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/mouseTarget.ts` so that clicks slightly left of the first character still resolve to column 1, or add a small left content padding. Related issue https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/93053 (editor left margin/gutter feature-request) is a complementary approach.
