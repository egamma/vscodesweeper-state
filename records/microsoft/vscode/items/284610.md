# Issue #284610 — Horizontal scrollbar persists even when overflowing line is fixed

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/284610](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/284610)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the horizontal scrollbar persists after editing a long line shorter because `_maxLineWidth` in viewLines.ts is only ever increased, never recalculated downward after line content changes.

## Change summary

Horizontal scrollbar remains visible after splitting or shortening a line that previously caused horizontal overflow, until a full re-render is triggered.

## Best solution

In `src/vs/editor/browser/viewParts/viewLines/viewLines.ts`, reset `_maxLineWidth` to 0 in `onLinesChanged` (and possibly `onLinesDeleted`/`onLinesInserted`) so that the next render pass recalculates the true max width. Care needed to avoid performance regression on every keystroke — a debounce or conditional reset (only when the changed line was the widest) may be appropriate.
