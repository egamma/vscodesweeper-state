# Issue #301836 — Audio cue plays while scrolling (copilot) chat history

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/301836](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/301836)

## Summary

Real bug — accessibility signals replay when chat content parts are re-created during scroll. The elicitation content part unconditionally calls acceptElicitation in its constructor (which plays chatUserActionRequired for pending elicitations), and question carousels without stable keys also lack replay guards. No fix has landed on main.

## Change summary

Scrolling through chat history replays audio cues (accessibility signals) as virtualized content parts are re-rendered.

## Best solution

Guard signal playback in ChatElicitationContentPart to not replay on re-render (similar to the _notifiedQuestionCarousels pattern in the list renderer). Ensure the question carousel guard also handles the undefined-stableKey case. Likely files: src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/chatContentParts/chatElicitationContentPart.ts and src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/chatListRenderer.ts.
