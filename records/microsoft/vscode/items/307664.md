## Summary

Team-authored bug that remains reproducible as of May 2026 despite the partial fix in PR #308629. The reporter confirmed on May 8 that stopping a session during a permission request (first message) still leaves a blank, stuck session in the list.

## Change summary

Stopping a new session during its first-turn permission request leaves an empty, stuck session in the session list that cannot be interacted with until after a window reload.

## Best solution

The fix in https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/pull/308629 addressed the archive/retention path but didn't cover the case where a session is stopped mid-permission-request before the first turn commits. The remaining gap is likely in the session lifecycle handling when cancellation arrives during a tool-approval wait — the session needs to either be fully disposed (removed from the list) or transitioned to a recoverable completed state immediately, not left in a blank limbo. Likely area: `src/vs/sessions/` session lifecycle and the `_sendFirstChat` cancellation path.
