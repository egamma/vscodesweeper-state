## Summary

Legitimate service-side content-filter false positive: the benign phrase 'tit-for-tat' triggers the Copilot backend's safety filter, suppressing all subsequent inline completions. The issue is already correctly labeled and routed to the inline-completions/quality team.

## Change summary

Copilot inline suggestions stop entirely after the phrase 'tit-for-tat' appears in a Markdown file, due to an overly aggressive server-side content filter.
