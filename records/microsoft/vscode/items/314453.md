# Issue #314453 — Draft Message Disappears When Switching Between Copilot Chat Tabs After Another Chat Completes

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/314453](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/314453)

## Summary

Draft text loss when switching between chat editor tabs after a background chat completes is a real bug in the chat draft persistence area. A team member has engaged, and the area has seen active fixes (draft sync flush, restoration logic) but the specific trigger — completion of a background chat causing draft loss on the active tab — may remain unaddressed. Keeping open for area owner.

## Change summary

Unsent draft message typed in one Copilot Chat editor tab disappears after switching away and back when another chat tab's request completes in the background.

## Best solution

Investigate the flush/restore lifecycle in `ChatInputPart.setInputModel` and `ChatWidget.setModel` when the editor pane switches tabs in the same group; the draft sync or the model-state autorun may lose the `inputText` field under the specific interleaving where a background model's request completes while a different model is active. Related work in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/input/chatInputPart.ts` and `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/chatWidget.ts`. See also https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/324149 (draft lost on new-chat switch) — the root cause may overlap.
