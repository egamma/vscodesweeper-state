[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/322583](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/322583)

## Summary

Plausible bug in terminal sticky scroll when a nested terminal (SSH/powershell) clears the screen, but the report lacks a screenshot or confirmation — requesting more info before triaging further.

## Change summary

When terminal sticky scroll is enabled and a nested terminal (SSH or powershell) clears the screen with Ctrl+L, the cursor is hidden behind the sticky scroll overlay.

## Best solution

Confirm the reproduction on latest Insiders with a screenshot, then evaluate whether the sticky scroll overlay should resize/hide when a nested shell clears the screen or when the cursor would be occluded. Likely related to how `TerminalStickyScrollOverlay` interacts with cursor position in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/terminalContrib/stickyScroll/browser/terminalStickyScrollOverlay.ts`. May share a root cause with https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/283335.
