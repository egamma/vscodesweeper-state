## Summary

Legitimate enterprise feature request for stronger MCP client assurance (DPoP, client attestation, policy controls). Overlaps significantly with #316625 (XAA/enterprise-managed auth) but asks for additional mechanisms like binary attestation and admin policy surfaces. Keeping open and routing to the relevant areas.

## Change summary

Add enterprise-grade MCP OAuth modes that provide stronger client assurance than a standard public native-app OAuth flow (DPoP, sender-constrained tokens, client attestation, admin policy controls).

## Best solution

Much of the enterprise-managed auth story is being addressed via https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/316625 (XAA/ID-JAG). The additional asks around client attestation, DPoP-bound tokens, and admin policy surfaces (allowlists of approved MCP servers/issuers) are reasonable enterprise hardening requests that could be tracked as follow-ups once the XAA foundation lands. The policy controls aspect may also intersect with VS Code's existing enterprise policy infrastructure.
