# Issue #298464 — Support adding hyphen and underscore when applying the kekab-case and snake_case actions

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/298464](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/298464)

## Summary

Valid feature request confirmed by source inspection — the snake_case and kebab-case transforms currently only handle camelCase boundaries but do not replace spaces. A maintainer has already greenlit a community PR attempt; keeping open for the community process.

## Change summary

"Transform to Snake Case" and "Transform to Kebab Case" should replace spaces with underscores or hyphens, respectively, in addition to splitting camelCase boundaries.

## Best solution

Add a `.replace(/\s+/g, '_')` (snake) / `.replace(/\s+/g, '-')` (kebab) step in the `_modifyText` methods of `SnakeCaseAction` and `KebabCaseAction` in `src/vs/editor/contrib/linesOperations/browser/linesOperations.ts`. A maintainer has already indicated openness to a PR.
