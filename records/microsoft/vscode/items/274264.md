Confirmed in source: VS Code hardcodes `'color: blue'` (#0000ff) for `[Extension Host]` console labels in `localProcessExtensionHost.ts`. Pure blue is notoriously unreadable on dark backgrounds. A brighter blue (e.g. `dodgerblue` / `cornflowerblue`) would fix the readability issue.
