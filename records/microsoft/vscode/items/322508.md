- **regex match confirmed:** The `plainTextPath` regex in `extensions/copilot/src/extension/linkify/common/filePathLinkifier.ts` (line 26) matches `./..` as a 4-char substring of `./...` due to greedy backtracking: the second `[^\s\`*${}()]+` first tries `..` (fails lookahead against trailing backtick), then backtracks to `.` (passes lookahead against the next `.`).
