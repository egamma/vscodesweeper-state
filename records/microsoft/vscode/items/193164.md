# Issue #193164 — Editor is broken when opening files with >120M lines

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/193164](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/193164)

## Summary

Still valid: the `| 0` int32 truncation in scrollable.ts (and elsewhere) remains on current main, so files with >120M lines still break scrolling. Low community traction (1 upvote) and niche scenario, but the technical analysis is sound and acknowledged by a team member.

## Change summary

Editor scrolling breaks for files exceeding ~120M lines because bitwise `| 0` truncates pixel heights above INT32_MAX to zero.

## Best solution

Either fail gracefully with a user-visible warning when scroll height would exceed INT32_MAX, or selectively replace `| 0` with `Math.trunc` in the scroll dimension paths — but the team needs to assess the performance tradeoff and real-world frequency (as alexdima suggested). Related exploration in https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/167719 (ArrayBuffer backend) could also inform the approach.
