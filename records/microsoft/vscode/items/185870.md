There is not enough concrete evidence of a fix on current main, nor a confirmed Chromium/Electron upstream issue to route or close it as upstream.

## Change summary

Editor ruler lines intermittently disappear or show gaps while scrolling large files with multiple configured rulers.

## Best solution

Keep this in `editor-core` and investigate the ruler rendering strategy in the editor view layer, likely by avoiding a single extremely tall ruler element and instead virtualizing or repositioning ruler drawing with scroll state if Chromium/GPU limits are confirmed.
