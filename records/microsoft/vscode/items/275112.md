# Issue #275112 — SCM - Error in dev console from scm view

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/275112](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/275112)

## Summary

Still reproduces on current main — the Throttler disposal race in scmHistoryViewPane.ts is unguarded. An open PR (#287184) exists but has not been merged. Keeping open for the area owner to review or land their own fix.

## Change summary

Autoruns in the SCM History View can fire after the view's Throttler instances are disposed, producing 'Throttler is disposed' console errors.

## Best solution

Guard `refresh()` and `_updateChildren()` in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/scm/browser/scmHistoryViewPane.ts` against post-disposal autorun firings — either with an `_isDisposed` flag (as PR #287184 proposes) or by moving the throttlers into the `_visibilityDisposables` store so they share the autorun lifecycle. The area owner (@lszomoru) should review/land the existing PR or apply their preferred fix.
