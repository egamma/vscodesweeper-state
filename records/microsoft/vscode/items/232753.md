## Change summary

Arrow keys in the accessible diff viewer of a notebook inline chat bubble navigate to adjacent cells instead of scrolling diff lines.

## Best solution

Replace the raw keydown listener (line 401–419 of accessibleDiffViewer.ts) with registered keybinding commands that have higher priority than `list.focusUp`/`list.focusDown`, so the diff viewer captures arrow keys before the notebook cell list does.
