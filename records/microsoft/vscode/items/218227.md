# Issue #218227 — SCM Graph - high contrast colors

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/218227](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/218227)

## Summary

Team-authored accessibility issue for SCM graph high contrast colors. The five lane colors (scmGraph.foreground1–5) still use flat hex values with no HC-specific overrides, and no dashed/dotted stroke patterns have been added. The work remains unaddressed on main.

## Change summary

The SCM graph swim-lane colors lack proper high-contrast theme support, making them hard to distinguish in HC Dark/Light themes.

## Best solution

Add hcDark/hcLight overrides for scmGraph.foreground1–5 in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/scm/browser/scmHistory.ts`, and consider alternating stroke patterns (dashed/dotted) for additional lanes as daviddossett suggested, since few colors offer enough contrast in HC themes. Related open issue https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/292708 covers the same gap and could be consolidated.
