## Summary

NES interfering with Tab-to-indent after paste is a real usability complaint confirmed by multiple users, but the team asked for a screen recording that was never provided. The confirmation-pending label and team engagement justify keeping this open rather than requesting info again.

## Change summary

NES accepts suggestions when the user presses Tab to indent after pasting, moving the cursor unexpectedly.

## Best solution

The NES inline completions controller should suppress or debounce suggestions immediately after a paste operation, or ensure Tab always indents when the user has just pasted and is adjusting indentation. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/editor/contrib/inlineCompletions/browser/controller/inlineCompletionsController.ts` or the trigger/acceptance logic.
