# Issue #256851 — Do not move my cursor while typing

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/256851](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/256851)

## Summary

Valid NES usability bug where Tab-to-indent conflicts with Tab-to-accept after paste. A team member asked for a screen recording (confirmation-pending), and multiple users confirmed the pattern. No fix found on main for suppressing NES after paste or disambiguating Tab intent in this scenario.

## Change summary

NES suggestions appear immediately after pasting code, causing Tab to accept a multi-line suggestion instead of indenting, which moves the cursor unexpectedly.

## Best solution

Suppress or debounce NES suggestions immediately after a paste operation, or require a non-Tab gesture to accept NES when the user is actively indenting. The fix likely belongs in `src/vs/editor/contrib/inlineCompletions/browser/` — either in the trigger/suppression logic or the accept-keybinding conditions.
