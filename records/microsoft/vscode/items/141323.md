## Change summary

Support the macOS Monterey fn+F (Globe+F) fullscreen shortcut in VS Code, which requires an Electron-level change.

## Best solution

The fix must land in Electron's `shell/browser/ui/cocoa/electron_menu_controller.mm` (mirroring the Chromium change at https://chromium-review.googlesource.com/c/chromium/src/+/3437253).
