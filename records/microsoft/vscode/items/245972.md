# Issue #245972 — Mac US International Keyboard auto closing quotes not working

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/245972](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/245972)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the composition path (used by dead-key input on US International keyboard) fails to over-type the auto-closed quote because intermediate compositionType events can invalidate the autoClosedCharacters tracking via _validateAutoClosedActions() before compositionEndWithInterceptors has a chance to use it.

## Change summary

With a US International keyboard on macOS, typing a closing quote via dead key + space inserts a duplicate quote instead of jumping over the auto-closed one.

## Best solution

Preserve autoClosedCharacters tracking during composition by either deferring _validateAutoClosedActions() until after compositionEnd processing, or by snapshotting autoClosedCharacters at composition start. The fix is localized to src/vs/editor/common/cursor/cursor.ts (the validation timing) and possibly cursorTypeOperations.ts.
