# Issue #166608 — "Go to bracket" jumps to random location on large files

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/166608](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/166608)

## Summary

Still reproducible on main: bracket pair matching uses `getLineTokens` without ensuring tokenization is complete, so on large files (where background tokenization hasn't finished), it produces incorrect results. The root cause identified by hediet remains unaddressed.

## Change summary

"Go to Bracket" jumps to wrong bracket positions on large files because bracket matching relies on incomplete tokenization state.

## Best solution

The bracket matching logic in `bracketPairsImpl.ts` needs to either force tokenization of the relevant range before matching, or detect incomplete tokenization and defer/warn. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/editor/common/model/bracketPairsTextModelPart/bracketPairsImpl.ts` (the `_matchBracket` method that calls `getLineTokens` without ensuring tokens are ready).
