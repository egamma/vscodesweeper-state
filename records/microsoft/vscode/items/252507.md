# Issue #252507 — Tab to accept inline (copilot) suggestion indents instead

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/252507](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/252507)

## Summary

Still a live design tension on current main: `_tabShouldIndent` (line 806 of inlineCompletionsModel.ts) returns true on empty lines, causing Tab to indent rather than accept an inline suggestion that starts with whitespace. No fix has landed; the `under-discussion` + `confirmation-pending` labels are appropriate.

## Change summary

When an inline Copilot suggestion appears on an empty line and itself starts with indentation, pressing Tab indents the line instead of accepting the suggestion.

## Best solution

Refine the `_tabShouldIndent` heuristic in `src/vs/editor/contrib/inlineCompletions/browser/model/inlineCompletionsModel.ts` to yield to `tabShouldAcceptInlineEdit` when a visible inline suggestion is present and starts at the cursor position — or adjust the `_inAcceptFlow` entry to cover the initial accept on an empty line. The design tradeoff (Tab-to-indent vs. Tab-to-accept) needs a product decision from the inline-completions owner.
