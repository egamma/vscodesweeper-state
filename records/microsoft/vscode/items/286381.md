# Issue #286381 — No clear indication that a thought can be expanded

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/286381](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/286381)

## Summary

This was addressed by PR #296621 (commit cca45c80c66), which added a hover chevron indicator to all collapsible content parts, including thinking titles. The chevron appears on hover when collapsed and stays visible when expanded, providing a clear affordance that the thought can be expanded.

## Change summary

Thought titles in chat responses lack a visual indicator (like a chevron/expando) that they are clickable and expandable.

## Best solution

Keep the shipped hover-chevron affordance from commit cca45c80c66 (1.110). The collapsible thinking header now shows a chevron-right on hover when collapsed and a persistent chevron-down when expanded, matching the ChatGPT-style expando pattern the issue requested.
