The chat accessibility service (`chatAccessibilityService.ts`) uses `status()` from the ARIA module to announce response content, and the streaming rendering pipeline may be emitting redundant ARIA live announcements. ## Best solution

The fix would ensure only the incremental new content is announced, not redundant or accumulated text. The accessible view (`chatResponseAccessibleView.ts`) may also contribute duplicate announcements.
