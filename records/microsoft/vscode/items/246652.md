## Change summary

DiffEditorGutter's menu observable recomputes after the scoped ContextKeyService is disposed, throwing 'AbstractContextKeyService has been disposed'.

## Best solution

Guard the gutter feature's menu observable against disposal — either dispose the menu/observable before the scoped CKS tears down, or add a disposal check in the `observableFromEvent` callback in `gutterFeature.ts`. The partial fix in 16f1ebc90d2 addresses a race in ScopedContextKeyService but the gutter's own lifecycle likely needs tightening.
