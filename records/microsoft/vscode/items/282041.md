# Issue #282041 — Very large line numbers show up in AI search results

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/282041](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/282041)

## Summary

Still present on main — AI search results display very large line numbers, likely because the AI search backend returns character/byte offsets rather than proper line:column ranges, and the rendering path in searchResultsView.ts displays them without validation. Author is a team member, so keeping open.

## Change summary

AI search results display very large, incorrect line numbers instead of meaningful line:column positions.

## Best solution

The AI search match ranges need to be validated or mapped from byte/character offsets to proper line:column ranges before display. The fix likely belongs in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/search/browser/AISearch/aiSearchModel.ts` (where raw results are processed into `AIFileMatch`) or in the match construction in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/search/browser/searchTreeModel/match.ts` (where `_documentRange` is converted to a `Range` with +1 offset). Either clamp/validate the range or convert the AI backend's offset-based ranges to line:column before they reach the renderer.
