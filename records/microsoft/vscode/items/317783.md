# Issue #317783 — too fast scrolling when drag&drop into editor (holding shift)

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/317783](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/317783)

## Summary

Active bug with a team member (alexdima) investigating. The scroll speed formula in `dragScrolling.ts` is proportional to viewport size and outside-distance, which can produce excessive speeds in small editor viewports. The issue is mid-investigation — keeping open.

## Change summary

Editor auto-scrolls uncontrollably fast during drag operations in small-viewport editors (split views, terminal open).

## Best solution

The `_getScrollSpeed()` in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/dragScrolling.ts` (lines 111–123) needs a speed cap or a damping factor for small viewports. Additionally, external drag-over scrolling (from explorer/sidebar) may need custom scroll logic since browser-native drag scroll isn't controllable. The maintainer's follow-up on sticky scroll settings may also be relevant.
