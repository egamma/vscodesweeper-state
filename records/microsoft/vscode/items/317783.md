# Issue #317783 — too fast scrolling when drag&drop into editor (holding shift)

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/317783](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/317783)

## Summary

Keep open and route to the editor area: the report has a concrete scenario plus a screen recording, and a maintainer confirmed the behavior looks bad. I did not find a fixing commit or duplicate evidence in the provided discussion or current checkout.

## Change summary

Dragging selected text or dropping into a small editor while holding Shift can trigger editor auto-scroll that starts too early and becomes too fast to control.

## Best solution

Editor maintainers should tune the drag auto-scroll thresholds/speed in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/dragScrolling.ts` and related mouse-position handling in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/mouseHandler.ts`, especially for small/split editor heights and Shift-assisted drops.
