# Issue #187027 — [wsl] support undo after deleting directory

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/187027](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/187027)

## Summary

Valid feature request for directory-level undo in WSL where no OS trash exists. A team member acknowledged the limitation without closing, suggesting it may be considered for future work despite the OS-level constraint.

## Change summary

Deleting a directory in WSL cannot be undone because WSL distros lack a Trash/Recycle Bin mechanism that VS Code relies on for undo.

## Best solution

Either implement a VS Code-managed soft-delete/trash for WSL remotes (similar to trash-cli or freedesktop trash spec), or document the limitation clearly and close as *as-designed. The team member's comment leans toward the latter, but they left it open — likely worth a product decision on whether to invest in a fallback trash mechanism for environments without native trash support.
