## Change summary

The macOS broker sign-in window shows the wordy title "Code - Insiders Helper (Plugin)" instead of just the product name, due to the CFBundleDisplayName value set in the Electron packaging.

## Best solution

When the team is ready to experiment, update the Electron packaging (in vscode-gulp-electron) to set CFBundleDisplayName to `opts.productName`.
