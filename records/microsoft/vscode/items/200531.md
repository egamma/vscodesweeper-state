# Issue #200531 — Click scroll bar by page not smooth(Editor,Workbench list )

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/200531](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/200531)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: clicking the scrollbar track with scrollByPage enabled always calls `setScrollPositionNow` (instant jump) instead of `setScrollPositionSmooth`, ignoring the smooth scrolling setting.

## Change summary

Clicking the scrollbar track to scroll by page ignores smooth scrolling settings, jumping instantly instead of animating.

## Best solution

In `src/vs/base/browser/ui/scrollbar/abstractScrollbar.ts`, the `_setDesiredScrollPositionNow` call at line 226 (the scrollByPage click handler) should use `setScrollPositionSmooth` when the scrollable has a non-zero smooth scroll duration. A new method or a flag could gate between smooth and immediate paths for scrollbar-track clicks.
