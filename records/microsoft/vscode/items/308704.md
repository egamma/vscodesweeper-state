Valid feature request for icon color support in QuickPick items, with team engagement and related open work in the same area. Keeping open as the request remains unaddressed on main.

## Change summary

QuickPick items do not render icon colors (e.g. ThemeIcon.color or symbol-icon colors) even though TreeView items do.

## Best solution

Extend QuickPick item rendering to support icon colors consistently with TreeView, likely touching the quick-input item renderer. Related work in https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/185356 (QuickInputButton colors) and https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/321773 (symbol codicon colors in quickpicks) may be addressed together.
