# Issue #180862 — Shift+Tab keybinding does not work in quick pick

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/180862](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/180862)

## Summary

Fixed on main and shipped in 1.105.0: the Tab handler that intercepted Shift+Tab in the quick pick was entirely removed in commit 4844cab9792, resolving this issue.

## Change summary

Shift+Tab keybinding in quick pick was intercepted by internal focus-wrapping code, preventing user-defined keybindings from working.

## Best solution

Keep the shipped fix (commit 4844cab9792, 1.105.0); no further change needed. The Tab/Shift+Tab handler was removed entirely from quickInputController.ts.
