Team-authored bug about corrupted terminal output after long-running Vite sessions on Windows, likely related to ConPTY resize handling. Re-opened and moved to Backlog by maintainers after offline discussion — keeping open per team-authored policy.

## Change summary

Terminal output from long-running Vite processes on Windows becomes visually broken/corrupted, possibly triggered by window or terminal resizing over time.

## Best solution

Investigate the ConPTY resize path for long-lived terminal sessions on Windows. The discussion suggests both the legacy ConPTY path (broken output after days) and the newer `windowsUseConptyDll` path (unusable experience with infinite ^C) have issues. The terminal-conpty area owner should try to reproduce with a long-running process and periodic resizes, and check whether ConPTY 1.25 improvements address either symptom.
