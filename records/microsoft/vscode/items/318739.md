# Issue #318739 — Copilot inline suggestions override or slow down snippets

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/318739](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/318739)

## Summary

This is a known consequence of the `editor.quickSuggestions.other` default being changed to `offWhenInlineCompletions` in commit ef680109cc3. The team is actively iterating on this area — subsequent fixes (3fcff1669e3, 95b4defad48) address some conflicts, and a team member is already engaged. Keeping open as part of the active work.

## Change summary

When Copilot inline suggestions are active, snippet/IntelliSense completions are either suppressed or delayed because the `offWhenInlineCompletions` default hides the suggest widget.

## Best solution

The team is actively balancing the interaction between inline completions and the suggest widget. Fixes on main (commit 3fcff1669e3 for the visual overlap, commit 95b4defad48 for triggering on disposed models) partially address this. The remaining gap — snippets being suppressed entirely when inline completions are showing — is the intended behavior of `offWhenInlineCompletions` and needs a product decision on how to surface snippets alongside ghost text.
