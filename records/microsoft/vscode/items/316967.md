# Issue #316967 — Session didn't detect the tunnel going offline

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/316967](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/316967)

## Summary

Team-authored bug report from testing: the agents/sessions window fails to detect and reflect tunnel offline state, and tunnel reconnection can leave a sticky auth error. Keeping open — filed by a MEMBER during test-plan execution.

## Change summary

The Sessions viewlet does not indicate when a tunnel goes offline, and reconnecting the tunnel does not always update the connection status; auth errors can become sticky across sessions.

## Best solution

The agents-window / remote-tunnel connectivity handling needs to detect tunnel offline events and surface them in the Sessions viewlet UI, and the auth/connection state needs to be properly reset on tunnel reconnection. Related issue https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/316979 tracks a similar 'No Host after disconnecting from tunnel' symptom and may share a root cause.
