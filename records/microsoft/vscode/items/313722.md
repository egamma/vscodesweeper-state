## Summary

This is a Copilot extension compaction bug, already labeled `chat-ext-issue`. Multiple fixes have landed on main since the report (token floor fix, stale compaction fix, Claude compact-and-continue fix), which likely address the described symptoms — but the issue should stay open under its current label for the extension team to confirm resolution and close.

## Change summary

Multi-turn agent sessions break on the second turn when the local tokenizer miscalculates context usage after a file-modification diff, triggering an infinite compaction loop.

## Best solution

The Copilot extension team should verify whether the fixes in commits 867957b9166 (token floor from server-reported prompt_tokens, May 9), 15bd7994c43 (stale background compaction guard, May 23), and 7551f35bacc (Claude compact-and-continue race fix, May 5) resolve this.
