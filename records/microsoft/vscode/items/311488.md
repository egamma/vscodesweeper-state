# Issue #311488 — Misaligned view title and actions in stacked panels (extra top offset on non-first views)

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/311488](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/311488)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: non-first pane headers get a 1px inline border-top (paneview.ts:396) within a box-sizing:border-box container, shrinking their content area by 1px relative to the first pane (whose border is removed by paneviewlet.css). The title/action buttons appear vertically offset.

## Change summary

Non-first view pane headers in stacked panels have a 1px vertical misalignment of title text and action buttons because their border-top reduces the content area.

## Best solution

Add 1px bottom-padding to non-first pane headers (or remove the top-padding from the first and apply a symmetric approach). The fix is localized to paneview.css or paneviewlet.css, ensuring the content centering is identical regardless of whether border-top is present.
