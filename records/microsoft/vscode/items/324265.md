  > Thanks for reaching out! This is a support question rather than a bug — Copilot completions are working as designed here.
  > 
  > To disable Copilot inline completions:
  > 1. Click the Copilot icon in the status bar (bottom right) and choose to disable completions, or
  > 2. Open Settings and set `github.copilot.editor.enableAutoCompletions` to `false`.
  > 
  > You can also dismiss individual suggestions by pressing Escape.
