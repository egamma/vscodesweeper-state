## Summary

A real and growing performance hotspot: font measurement in `charWidthReader.ts` triggers expensive synchronous DOM layout reads during display-change events, affecting ~19k users in 1.121.0. The Dec 2025 throttle (commit 610c5b09c0d) mitigates rapid-fire events but doesn't reduce the per-invocation cost. Keeping open for the editor team to evaluate further optimization.

## Change summary

The `_readFromDomElements` font measurement path causes expensive forced layout/reflow, showing up as a PerfSampleError hotspot with rapidly growing user impact across stable releases.

## Best solution

Optimize or defer the synchronous DOM measurement in `charWidthReader.ts` — e.g. batch reads away from the layout-triggering writes, use `requestAnimationFrame` scheduling, or cache measurements more aggressively. The editor/font-measurement area owns this (`src/vs/editor/browser/config/charWidthReader.ts`, `fontMeasurements.ts`).
