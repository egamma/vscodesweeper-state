# Issue #148191 — Notebook lightbulb action should stay around after clicking

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/148191](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/148191)

## Summary

The lightbulb still disappears immediately after clicking because the status bar provider only shows it when currentLanguageId !== guess (line 138 of statusBarProviders.ts). The requested 'sticky/cycling' behavior has not been implemented.

## Change summary

Request for the notebook language-detection lightbulb to remain visible after clicking, allowing users to cycle through other detected languages or undo the selection.

## Best solution

Extend CellStatusBarLanguageDetectionProvider to keep the lightbulb visible after acceptance (e.g. until the cell is next executed), and optionally expose a cycling command through the ranked detection results. The change is localized to src/vs/workbench/contrib/notebook/browser/contrib/cellStatusBar/statusBarProviders.ts and the related detect-cell-language command.
