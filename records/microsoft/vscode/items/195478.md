# Issue #195478 — Anoying notification when no workspace opened

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/195478](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/195478)

## Summary

A team member (connor4312, the js-debug maintainer) engaged directly, implying this may be valid feedback for js-debug to silently skip envFile resolution when no workspace is open. Keeping open for the area owner's decision.

## Change summary

Setting debug.javascript.terminalOptions with an envFile referencing ${workspaceFolder} produces a noisy notification when no workspace folder is open.

## Best solution

The js-debug extension should silently skip envFile resolution when ${workspaceFolder} cannot be resolved (no open workspace) rather than showing an error notification. The fix belongs in the vscode-js-debug repo's env-file resolution path.
