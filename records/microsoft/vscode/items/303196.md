## Summary

Confirmed on current main: getWorkerBootstrapUrl at src/vs/platform/webWorker/browser/webWorkerServiceImpl.ts lines 83–94 has the described bugs — raw workerScriptUrl is used in the template literal without stripping query/fragment, and lastIndexOf('#', start) misbehaves when start is -1.
