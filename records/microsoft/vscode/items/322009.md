# Issue #322009 — Chats in history might change order and seem to (or truly) disappear and reappear

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/322009](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/322009)

## Summary

Team-authored issue — keeping open. The sorting logic in AgentSessionsSorter intentionally prioritizes NeedsInput sessions to the top within groups, which causes sessions to jump position when their status changes, then return when they complete. This is likely the root cause of the perceived disappearance/reappearance.

## Change summary

Chat sessions in the history sidebar unexpectedly change sort order, appearing to disappear and reappear, when their status transitions (e.g., to NeedsInput or InProgress).

## Best solution

The `AgentSessionsSorter.compare()` in `agentSessionsViewer.ts` prioritizes active/NeedsInput sessions to the top, causing position jumps on status transitions. Consider either (a) stabilizing sort position so sessions don't move once placed in a date group, or (b) adding visual affordances (animation, highlight) when a session moves due to status change so users understand what happened. The grouping into date sections ("OLDER") compounds the confusion when a session jumps across group boundaries.
