# Issue #194902 — Substitution performed when accepting a Kanji conversion of Japanese input method

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/194902](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/194902)

## Summary

Fixed on main: the Replace Enter keybinding now requires `isComposing` to be false, preventing replacement from triggering while accepting an IME composition. Shipped in 1.109.0.

## Change summary

Pressing Enter to accept a Kanji conversion in the Find & Replace widget also triggered the replace action.
