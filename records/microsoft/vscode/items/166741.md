## Change summary

Sticky scroll causes markdown preview/editor scroll sync to stay offset until the final heading because occluded sticky lines are still treated as visible editor lines.

## Best solution

Track this in the Backlog under the existing markdown/editor sticky scroll ownership; the best end state is for editor visible range or reveal behavior to expose enough sticky-scroll-aware information for markdown preview sync without extensions guessing from `editor.stickyScroll.maxLineCount`.
