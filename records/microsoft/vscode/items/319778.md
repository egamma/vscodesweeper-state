Valid feature request to increase the maximum allowed value of `scm.diffDecorationsGutterWidth` from 5 to ~10. No technical constraint prevents this — just an arbitrary enum cap. Keeping open for community traction.

## Change summary

Straightforward change with no architectural concern.
