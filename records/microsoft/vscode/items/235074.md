[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/235074](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/235074)

## Summary

Team-authored bug report: the paste widget (PostEditWidget) in the chat panel persists after undo. The PostEditWidgetManager does subscribe to onDidChangeModelContent to clear the widget (src/vs/editor/contrib/dropOrPasteInto/browser/postEditWidget.ts:168), but there may be an ordering or re-entrance issue specific to the chat input editor. Keeping open as a team-filed issue.

## Change summary

After pasting code into the chat panel and pressing undo, the paste options widget remains visible instead of being dismissed.

## Best solution

Investigate why `onDidChangeModelContent` in `PostEditWidgetManager` (postEditWidget.ts:168) does not clear the widget on undo in the chat input editor. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/editor/contrib/dropOrPasteInto/browser/postEditWidget.ts` or the chat input setup in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/input/chatInputPart.ts`. It may need an explicit undo listener or a guard in the clear/show sequencing.
