# Issue #191024 — Consider moving `NotebookSearchService` to be called with `ISearchService` or related service

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/191024](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/191024)

## Summary

Team-authored internal refactoring request (MEMBER). The separation still exists on current main — `INotebookSearchService` is called alongside `ISearchService` in `searchModel.ts` rather than through a unified entry point. Keeping open for the area owner.

## Change summary

Unify notebook and text search behind a single entry point (e.g. a central `textSearchAllTypes()`) instead of requiring callers to coordinate both services separately.

## Best solution

Integrate notebook search results into `ISearchService` (or a thin wrapper) so callers don't orchestrate `INotebookSearchService` separately. The coordination logic in `SearchModel.doSearch()` (`src/vs/workbench/contrib/search/browser/searchTreeModel/searchModel.ts`, ~line 172) is the main call-site to consolidate.
