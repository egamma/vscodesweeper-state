# Issue #185881 — Restarting the pty host will sometimes dispose of the new processes

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/185881](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/185881)

## Summary

Race condition in pty host restart that sometimes disposes newly-created processes. Multiple related fixes have landed (connection restoration, direct proxy cleanup, disposable tracking) but this specific intermittent race remains open with active investigation by a team member in Dec 2025.

## Change summary

Restarting the pty host sometimes races with new terminal process creation, causing newly spawned processes to be disposed and the terminal panel to close.

## Best solution

The race likely lives in the timing between `_onPtyHostRestart` firing, the old direct proxy being cleared (via `_directProxyDisposables`), and new processes being attached to the old proxy before the new one connects. The fix should ensure new process attachments are deferred or queued until the new pty host connection is established. Active investigation by anthonykim1 — keep open.
