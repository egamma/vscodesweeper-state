## Summary

Team-authored feedback on the agents window chat UX — the 'Done' button appears functionally identical to 'Delete', suggesting one should be renamed or differentiated. Keeping open for maintainer judgment.

## Change summary

The 'Done' button in an agent conversation appears to do the same thing as 'Delete' (removes the chat), causing confusion about their distinction.

## Best solution

Clarify the semantic difference between 'Done' and 'Delete' in the agents window chat UI — either rename 'Delete' to 'Done' if they are truly equivalent, or differentiate their behavior (e.g., 'Done' archives/completes while 'Delete' permanently removes). Likely lives in `src/vs/sessions/` or `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/`.
