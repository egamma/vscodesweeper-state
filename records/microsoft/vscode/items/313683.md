## Summary

Actively being worked on by a maintainer. The `explorer_command` DLL part was fixed in commit c2a42605f78 (merged May 6, 2026). The `code-tunnel-insiders.exe` file-lock error during update remains open per dmitrivMS's comment.

## Change summary

Windows updater fails with file-in-use errors for `code-tunnel-insiders.exe` and `code_insider_explorer_command_x64.dll` during self-update.

## Best solution

The `explorer_command` DLL lock is resolved by terminating the COM surrogate before update/gc (c2a42605f78). The remaining `code-tunnel-insiders.exe` lock still needs investigation — likely the tunnel process isn't being stopped before the updater tries to rename it. The fix probably belongs in `build/win32/code.iss` or `src/vs/platform/update/electron-main/updateService.win32.ts`.
