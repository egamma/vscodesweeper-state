## Summary

Team-authored follow-up from PR #279249: in a local empty workspace, the terminal is blocked by workspace trust logic even though local terminals should be allowed to open. Hard keep-open rule applies (MEMBER author).

## Change summary

Allow a local terminal to open in an empty workspace without requiring workspace trust confirmation.
