# Issue #264741 — Can't get suggestions after clearing the terminal

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/264741](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/264741)

## Summary

Team-authored bug that remains unfixed on main — terminal suggestions break after clearing with Ctrl+L or after Ctrl+Backspace. Multiple team members have confirmed the issue persists intermittently.

## Change summary

Terminal suggest completions stop appearing after clearing the terminal with Ctrl+L or after deleting an inserted suggestion with Ctrl+Backspace.

## Best solution

Investigate the terminal suggest addon's state management after a clear event (Ctrl+L) — likely the suggest trigger detection or completion cache gets into a stale state when the terminal buffer is cleared without a new prompt being emitted. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/terminalContrib/suggest/`.
