# Issue #244021 — Auto-indent of switch-case is idiotic

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/244021](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/244021)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main. PHP's indentation rules in language-configuration.json don't handle the decrease-indent after `break;` for the next `case` in curly-brace switch syntax. The tree-sitter autoindent work (acknowledged by maintainer) has not yet landed for PHP.

## Change summary

Auto-indent incorrectly increases indentation for each successive `case` in a PHP switch statement instead of keeping them at the same level.

## Best solution

The proper fix is through tree-sitter-based auto-indentation (as labeled). In the interim, the `decreaseIndentPattern` in `extensions/php/language-configuration.json` could potentially be extended to handle `break;` followed by `case`, but the regex-based system has fundamental limitations for this pattern. The tree-sitter approach is the right long-term fix.
