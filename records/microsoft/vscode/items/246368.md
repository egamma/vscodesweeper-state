## Summary

Confirmed bug: extension marketplace images fail to load on vscode.dev due to CORS restrictions in the webview iframe context. Team member sandy081 confirmed it reproduces on vscode.dev even without tunnels. No fix has landed.

## Change summary

Extension detail/preview tab images show CORS errors (broken icons) when viewed on vscode.dev.

## Best solution

The webview rendering extension READMEs (in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/extensions/browser/extensionEditor.ts`) needs a mechanism to proxy or handle cross-origin image loads in the web context — likely a service-worker approach or adding `crossorigin` attributes, or proxying images through the vscode.dev backend. The `extensions` area team owns this.
