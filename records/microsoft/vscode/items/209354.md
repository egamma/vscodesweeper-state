# Issue #209354 — Terminal buttons pushed outside of the app window boundary when resizing the panel

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/209354](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/209354)

## Summary

Confirmed by a team member (benibenj) as reproducible — the composite bar in the panel title sometimes fails to collapse left-aligned tabs into the overflow menu when resized, pushing right-aligned toolbar buttons offscreen. The intermittent nature and profile-dependent behavior suggest a layout timing issue in `layoutCompositeBar`. No fix commit found on main.

## Change summary

Panel title bar tabs don't always collapse into the overflow ellipsis when the panel is narrowed, causing right-aligned action buttons to be pushed outside the visible window boundary.

## Best solution

Investigate the timing/ordering of `layoutCompositeBar()` vs toolbar rendering in `src/vs/workbench/browser/parts/paneCompositePart.ts` — when `getToolbarWidth()` is called before the toolbar items are measured (returns 0), the composite bar gets too much space and the toolbar overflows. The fix likely needs an additional relayout trigger after toolbar items finish rendering, or a CSS-level `overflow: hidden` / `flex-shrink` guard on the composite-bar container.
