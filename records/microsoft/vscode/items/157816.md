# Issue #157816 — three space block does not indent with tab

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/157816](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/157816)

## Summary

This is the intended behavior of the `useTabStops` setting (enabled by default): Tab snaps to the next tab stop rather than adding a full indent. When a line is at 3 spaces in a 4-space file, Tab aligns it to 4 rather than adding 4. The issue is a design discussion about whether multi-line indent should behave differently.

## Change summary

Pressing Tab on a multi-line selection containing a misaligned (3-space) line snaps that line to the next tab stop instead of adding a full indent level uniformly.

## Best solution

This could remain as-designed (the workaround is disabling `editor.useTabStops`), or the shift command in `src/vs/editor/common/commands/shiftCommand.ts` could be updated to treat multi-line selections differently from single-line tab-stop snapping. Needs a product decision on whether uniformity across the selection should trump per-line tab-stop alignment.
