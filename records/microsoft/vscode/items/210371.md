## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the default `markSectionHeaderRegex` captures everything after `MARK:` to end-of-line including trailing comment delimiters (`-->`, `*/`, `--->`) in the `label` group. PR #308930 proposes a fix but is not yet merged.
