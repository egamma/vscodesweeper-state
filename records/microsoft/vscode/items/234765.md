# Issue #234765 — When few QuickPickItems are shown there is not enough contrast in dark mode

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/234765](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/234765)

## Summary

Still valid on current main: the Dark Modern theme's quick pick background (#222222) lacks sufficient visual separation from the dark editor background when few items are shown. Recent contrast work (2026 themes) doesn't address Dark Modern. A team member already routed this to a designer.

## Change summary

In Dark Modern theme, a quick pick with few items blends into the dark editor background due to insufficient shadow/border contrast.

## Best solution

Improve the quick pick widget's visual boundary in Dark Modern — either strengthen the box-shadow for dark themes, add a subtle border, or lighten the quick pick background slightly. The 2026 themes work (commit 1309e8018c2) shows the pattern for addressing this kind of contrast issue. Likely touched in `extensions/theme-defaults/themes/dark_modern.json` or in the quick pick shadow CSS.
