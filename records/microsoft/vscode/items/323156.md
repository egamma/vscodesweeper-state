## Summary

Real regression in 1.126.0 (60× spike): `$isFileIgnored` throws when the extension-host receives an RPC call for a `LanguageModelIgnoredFileProvider` handle that was already unregistered. An open fix PR exists — keep open pending its review and merge.

## Change summary

## Best solution
