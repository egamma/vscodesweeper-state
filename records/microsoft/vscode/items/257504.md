[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/257504](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/257504)

## Summary

Valid, properly-triaged feature request for NES word wrap support. Word wrap is intentionally disabled in the inline edits preview editor (commit 1b078e1a20c). This is the canonical issue — #264411 was closed as its duplicate. 12 upvotes, not yet at the 20-upvote Backlog promotion threshold.

## Change summary

NES inline edit suggestions do not respect word wrap, making long-line diffs unreadable or cropped.

## Best solution

Enable word wrap in the NES inline edits preview editor (currently hardcoded to 'off' in `inlineEditsSideBySideView.ts`), likely respecting the user's `editor.wordWrap` setting. The fix touches the preview editor options in the side-by-side view widget.
