# Issue #248606 — Screen reader repeats last word twice at end of line and on empty lines with Ctrl+Arrow

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248606](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248606)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the screen reader content strategy does not produce correct word-boundary announcements for Ctrl+Arrow navigation at end-of-line or on empty lines. No fix found in git history.

## Change summary

Screen reader repeats the last word when Ctrl+Right reaches end-of-line and on subsequent empty lines, instead of announcing 'end of line' or 'blank'.

## Best solution

The screen reader content update logic in `src/vs/editor/browser/controller/editContext/native/screenReaderContentSimple.ts` (and the `SimplePagedScreenReaderStrategy` in `screenReaderUtils.ts`) needs to detect word-boundary cursor stops at EOL and empty lines, and provide differentiated content so screen readers announce correctly. May require coordination with how Chromium/EditContext exposes selection changes to assistive technology.
