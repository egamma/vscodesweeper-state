## Summary

Valid request to improve diff algorithm post-processing for better line matching when lists differ greatly in size. Already acknowledged by hediet (diff editor owner) and properly triaged in Backlog. The recent @vscode/diff adoption may or may not have addressed this — keeping open.

## Change summary

Diff editor fails to match identical lines (HEIF, VP9, WebP) when comparing a short list against a much longer one, showing everything as a single replacement instead.

## Best solution

Improve the diff post-processing heuristics (likely in @vscode/diff or its integration) to prefer aligning identical lines even when the surrounding context is heavily changed. The recent adoption of the external @vscode/diff package (commit 5fc781f799f) may provide a better path for tuning this.
