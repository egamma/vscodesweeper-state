Team-authored UX debt issue about the terminal tab selection indicator being visually obscured by focus outlines and selection highlights. Keeping open — filed by a team member and describes a real visual polish concern.

## Change summary

The blue bar indicating the selected terminal tab is covered by the focus outline and selection highlight, making it hard to see which terminal is active.

## Best solution

Adjust the terminal tab styling so the active-tab indicator (the blue bar) remains visible when the tab area receives focus or when a tab is clicked. This likely involves CSS changes in the terminal tabs component (`src/vs/workbench/contrib/terminal/browser/terminalTabbedView.ts` or related CSS) to ensure the selection indicator has visual precedence over focus/selection states.
