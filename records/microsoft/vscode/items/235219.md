## Summary

Team-authored issue; the GPU renderer still lacks specific handling for folding expand decorations (the '...' inline decoration after a collapsed line). No fix found on main — the bug remains open.

## Change summary

With GPU acceleration enabled, folding collapse markers ('...') persist after expanding and the line renders with visual artifacts (bold/fuzzy text).

## Best solution

The GPU renderer's inline decoration pipeline needs to properly support the folding expand widget lifecycle — rendering the dots on collapse, removing them on expand, and avoiding double-rendering artifacts. Likely involves `src/vs/editor/browser/gpu/renderStrategy/` and the decoration handling in `viewGpuContext.ts`.
