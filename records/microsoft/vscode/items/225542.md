# Issue #225542 — Esc closes the attach picker and doesn't go back to quick chat

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/225542](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/225542)

## Summary

Team-authored bug report about ESC in the attach picker dismissing quick chat entirely instead of returning focus to it. No fix found on main; keeping open per team-authored policy.

## Change summary

Pressing Escape in the attachment picker (paperclip) within Quick Chat closes the entire Quick Chat instead of returning to it.

## Best solution

The quick chat's `onDidHide` handler (src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widgetHosts/chatQuick.ts line 121) fires when the attach picker's quick pick closes, tearing down the whole quick chat. The fix likely involves distinguishing a nested picker dismiss from a top-level dismiss — either by suppressing the `onDidHide` while a child picker is open, or by re-showing the quick chat after the attach picker closes.
