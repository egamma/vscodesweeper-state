## Change summary

The Allow/Allow All button in the chat tool confirmation carousel is hidden or obscured, preventing users from approving tool invocations and blocking the chat session.

## Best solution

Investigate the layout/overflow behavior of the confirmation carousel in `src/vs/workbench/contrib/chat/browser/widget/chatContentParts/toolInvocationParts/chatToolConfirmationCarouselPart.ts` and its CSS in `chatToolConfirmationCarousel.css`. The `max-height: min(300px, 45vh)` constraint with `overflow: hidden` on `.chat-tool-confirmation-carousel` may clip the Allow button when the confirmation content is tall. The `.chat-buttons-container` has `flex-shrink: 0` but may still be pushed out of view.
