# Issue #248760 — NES: InlineInsertion renders over the current line with editor.scrollBeyondLastLine

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248760](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248760)

## Summary

Still appears unfixed on main: the ghost text AdditionalLinesWidget view zone can detach from its anchor line when scrollBeyondLastLine is active near the end of the file, causing the insertion to render over the wrong line. A team member acknowledged the bug and invited a community fix.

## Change summary

NES multiline ghost text renders over the current line instead of between lines when editor.scrollBeyondLastLine is enabled near EOF.

## Best solution

Fix the view zone positioning in the AdditionalLinesWidget (ghostTextView.ts) to account for the extra scroll space added by scrollBeyondLastLine, ensuring the zone stays anchored to its afterLineNumber even when the user scrolls in the beyond-last-line area.
