# Issue #248760 — NES: InlineInsertion renders over the current line with editor.scrollBeyondLastLine

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248760](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/248760)

## Summary

Still present on main: the AdditionalLinesWidget view zone for NES ghost text insertions can visually detach from its anchor line when scrollBeyondLastLine is active and the suggestion appears near the end of the file. The view zone positioning logic has no special handling for the scroll-beyond-last-line padding area.

## Change summary

NES inline insertion (ghost text) rendered as additional lines floats away from the cursor line when scrolling with editor.scrollBeyondLastLine enabled.

## Best solution

The fix belongs in `AdditionalLinesWidget.addViewZone` — the view zone needs to account for the scroll-beyond-last-line virtual space so it stays visually anchored to the content line during scrolling. This may require coordination with the editor's scroll-top computation when view zones are placed after the last content line.
