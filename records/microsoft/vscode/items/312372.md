## Summary

Real race condition still present on main: the CommandsConverter cache entry is disposed when the changesView autorun re-evaluates and recreates the MenuWorkbenchButtonBar, but a click arriving in the brief disposal window finds no cached command. Very low impact (1 user, 1 hit).

## Change summary

Clicking a toolbar button in the Agents changes view can throw 'command not found' if the button bar is disposed and recreated by an autorun at the exact moment of the click.

## Best solution

Guard the _executeConvertedCommand path in extHostCommands.ts to gracefully handle a missing cache entry (e.g., return a no-op or re-resolve), or restructure the changesView autorun to defer disposal of the old button bar until the new one is fully wired. The fix belongs in either src/vs/workbench/api/common/extHostCommands.ts (defensive) or src/vs/sessions/contrib/changes/browser/changesView.ts (preventive).
