## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the terminal suggest status bar titles and tooltips hardcode "Tab" and "Tab or Enter" instead of resolving the user's keybinding customizations. The PR #286185 was closed without merging.

## Change summary

Terminal suggest selection-mode status bar labels and tooltips hardcode "Tab" instead of reflecting the user's custom keybinding for acceptSelectedSuggestion.
