SCMRepository receives an external DisposableStore and disposes it itself, violating the convention that objects should manage their own disposables.

## Best solution

Refactor SCMRepository to extend Disposable (or implement IDisposable with its own internal DisposableStore), moving the disposables currently added externally into the class itself. The change is localized to src/vs/workbench/contrib/scm/common/scmService.ts.
