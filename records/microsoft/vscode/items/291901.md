# Issue #291901 — Out of bounds text selection behaves weird

## Summary

Keep open as a plausible editor-core wrapping bug; the report has clear steps, extensions-disabled confirmation, and no duplicate/fix evidence in the issue context or local history.

## Change summary

Mouse selection dragged past the right edge of a wrapped editor line can jump into the next wrapped segment instead of staying at the nearest visual text edge.

## Best solution

Investigate the right/outside-editor mouse-selection path in src/vs/editor/browser/controller/mouseHandler.ts and src/vs/editor/browser/controller/dragScrolling.ts, then clamp the drag selection endpoint to the wrapped view-line boundary and cover it with an editor mouse/wrapping regression test.
