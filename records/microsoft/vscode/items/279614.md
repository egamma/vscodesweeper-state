## Summary

Fixed on main by commit 28704d8c988, which addresses stale OSC 8 link hover tooltips — specifically adding a `leave` handler and invalidating the hover on viewport changes. Shipped in 1.124.0.

## Change summary

OSC 8 hyperlink tooltip reappears (resurrects) when mousing over content containing OSC 633 sequences after leaving the link.

## Best solution

Keep the shipped fix in commit 28704d8c988 (1.124.0). The hover is now properly dismissed on leave and invalidated on render/scroll, preventing the resurrection behavior.
