# Issue #311971 — inlayHints and documentSymbols are requested twice on every key press + inlayHints are called again on every show

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/311971](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/311971)

## Summary

Confirmed performance bug: inlayHints are requested twice per keystroke (one from immediate schedule, one from a delayed timeout in the same content-change handler) and again on Ctrl+Alt modifier events even without document changes. DocumentSymbols are also double-requested from separate outline/breadcrumbs instances. No fix on current main.

## Change summary

InlayHints and documentSymbols providers are called twice on every content change, and inlayHints additionally re-fetches on Ctrl+Alt modifier key presses without document changes.

## Best solution

For inlayHints: the `onDidChangeModelContent` handler in `inlayHintsController.ts` should not both `scheduler.schedule()` immediately AND set a delayed `disposableTimeout(() => scheduler.schedule(0), delay)` — the delayed one should be the only trigger. The modifier-key handler (lines 209–221) should skip `scheduler.schedule(0)` when hints are already cached and the document hasn't changed. For documentSymbols: investigate why the `OutlineModelService` cache doesn't prevent duplicate provider calls when outline pane and breadcrumbs fire their debounced timers independently.
