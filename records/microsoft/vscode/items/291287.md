# Issue #291287 — Thinking block scroll bar flashes on every update

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/291287](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/291287)

## Summary

Still reproduces on main: the thinking block's scrollbar is configured with `ScrollbarVisibility.Auto` and every streaming update triggers `setScrollDimensions`, causing the scrollbar to flash in and out. No fix has landed for this specific flashing behavior.

## Change summary

The scrollbar in the thinking block flashes on every content update during streaming, creating a distracting visual artifact.

## Best solution

Suppress scrollbar visibility transitions during active streaming in `chatThinkingContentPart.ts` — either by temporarily switching to `ScrollbarVisibility.Hidden` while auto-scrolling, debouncing dimension updates to coalesce rapid changes, or using CSS to suppress the scrollbar fade animation during streaming.
