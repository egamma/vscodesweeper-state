Team-authored feature request for actioning session widgets (e.g. approvals) directly from the session hover in the Agents window. Active design discussion between team members; must stay open per keep-open rules.

Allow users to take actions (like approvals) on session widgets directly within the session hover, rather than needing to open the full session.
