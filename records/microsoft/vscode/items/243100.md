# Issue #243100 — Automatic indentation doesn't work well with C/C++

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/243100](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/243100)

## Summary

Still open on main — the auto-indentation logic does not account for consecutive Enter presses after braceless C/C++ control structures. The issue has 22 upvotes, is in Backlog, and is tagged as a tree-sitter autoindent candidate. Team member aiday-mar is actively engaged.

## Change summary

Pressing Enter twice after a braceless `if` statement in C/C++ produces incorrect indentation on the second blank line.

## Best solution

The indentation engine needs to track `indentNextLinePattern` state across consecutive Enter presses (not just the immediate previous non-empty line). The `tree-sitter-autoindent-candidate` label suggests this may be addressed by the tree-sitter-based indentation work in progress. The fix likely lives in `src/vs/editor/common/languages/autoIndent.ts` and the C/C++ language configuration's `indentNextLinePattern` handling.
