The issue describes a sporadic naming problem with devcontainer workspaces in the Recently Opened list. A team member acknowledged it needs investigation but no fix was found on main. The intermittent nature makes source-only verification impractical — keeping open.

## Change summary

Dev Container workspace entries in the 'Recently Opened' list sporadically lose their proper names and show as 'workspace [Dev Container] /' instead.

## Best solution

Investigate the label formatter registration timing for remote/devcontainer workspaces in `src/vs/workbench/services/label/common/labelService.ts` — the label formatters contributed by the remote extension may not yet be registered when the recently-opened list renders, causing a fallback label. A fix would ensure formatters are awaited or the list re-renders when formatters register.
