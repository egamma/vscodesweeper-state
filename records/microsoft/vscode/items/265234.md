Intermittent auto-scroll regression in agent-mode chat, confirmed by a team member (zhichli) but not yet reliably reproducible or fixed. Keeping open — the bug is real but hard to pin down, and a hard needs-info ask would be counterproductive given the team-member confirmation.

## Change summary

Chat panel in Agent mode sometimes auto-scrolls back to a 'Restore Checkpoint' position mid-conversation, interrupting the user's reading flow.

## Best solution

Investigate the auto-scroll and reveal logic in `chatListWidget.ts` and `chatWidget.ts` — the checkpoint container visibility toggle (line ~970 in `chatListRenderer.ts`) or `scrollToCurrentItem`/`scrollToEnd` race conditions during layout may be causing spurious reveals. Possibly related to https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/293106 which tracks similar scroll-jump regressions; if that issue absorbs this scenario, consolidate there.
