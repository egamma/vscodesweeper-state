## Summary

Valid feature request for a zenMode.hideBreadcrumbs setting — not yet implemented on main, has team engagement and community interest. Keeping open for the community process.

## Change summary

Add a setting to hide breadcrumbs specifically in Zen Mode, analogous to existing zenMode.hideStatusBar and zenMode.hideActivityBar.
