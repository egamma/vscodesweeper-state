## Summary

Team-filed visual bug: the scrollbar's fade-out transition causes it to render behind a collapsed editor section, likely a z-index stacking issue between the scrollbar (z-index: 11 when visible, removed when invisible) and the collapsed section overlay. Keeping open per team-authored rule.
