# Issue #210573 — Resizing Primary Side Bar opens terminal Panel

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/210573](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/210573)

## Summary

Confirmed and still present on main: the grid splitview's snap mechanism treats the hidden panel as a snap-after candidate when the sidebar on the same side is resized, causing the panel to snap visible unintentionally.

## Change summary

Resizing the Primary Side Bar unexpectedly opens the hidden Panel when both are positioned on the same side.

## Best solution

The grid's snap logic in splitview.ts (findFirstSnapIndex / resize) needs to distinguish between intentional snap targets (the view being directly resized) and incidental neighbors. When a sash drag on the sidebar triggers a snap, the panel should not be eligible as a snap candidate unless the user is directly interacting with the panel's own sash boundary.
