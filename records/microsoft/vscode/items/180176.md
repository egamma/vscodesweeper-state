## Summary

Still valid: the inline suggestion signal reuses the shared `quickFixes.mp3` sound rather than having its own dedicated less-disruptive audio. The sound file was updated in Jan 2025 but the fundamental request for a unique, lighter sound remains unaddressed.

Replace the inline suggestion audio cue with a less disruptive, lower-energy sound distinct from the quickFixes signal.
