# Issue #201431 — [folding] Manual folding range not useful after copy-paste

[https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/201431](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/issues/201431)

## Summary

Still valid on main — manual folding ranges created to preserve collapsed state become stale/incorrect after paste operations, and no logic exists to invalidate them. Acknowledged by maintainer as a real issue with a workaround (Remove Manual Folding Ranges command).

## Change summary

After copy-paste, previously collapsed manual folding ranges persist and produce incorrect/useless fold markers on the pasted content.

## Best solution

When content is pasted over a region, invalidate any manual folding ranges whose line spans were replaced. The logic likely belongs in `src/vs/editor/contrib/folding/browser/foldingModel.ts` where manual ranges are tracked and reapplied after edits.
